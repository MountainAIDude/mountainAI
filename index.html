# ColossusNorth · built in the cold for a hot compute age

The Plan

Turn a flare-wasted, under-used energy basin into a negative-carbon AI and HPC engine that heats real homes and communities.

  • 100 % off-grid power (stranded flares + geothermal co-production).
  • PUE target 1.04 using −40 °C free air plus full heat recovery.
  • All waste heat contracted into district heating loops for nearby communities.
  • First Nations carried interest and revenue share written in from day zero.
  • Zero grid dependency by design; local resilience first.
