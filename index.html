What we are building

A new class of compute campus that treats power, cooling, and community as one integrated system.

Energy + Power

Off-grid generation with long-term supply agreements, optimized for cold-climate reliability and low-cost power.

Compute Campus

Modular data halls and networking built for high-density AI workloads, phased from pilot to full scale.

Heat Reuse

Waste heat captured for adjacent industrial, commercial, and hospitality uses to maximize total efficiency.

Status: early stage / site diligence underway

Infrastructure & location

Access to underutilized industrial land, workforce capacity, and existing corridors to support heavy buildout.

Cold ambient temperatures reduce cooling load while enabling stable year-round operations.

Positioned to deliver some of the most efficient AI compute in North America while creating local energy reuse opportunities.
